# Frequently Asked Questions

## About the Community

**What is Istanbul Digital Nomads?**
We're a community of remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads who live in or visit Istanbul. We organize meetups, share resources, and help each other navigate life in the city.

**How do I join?**
Join our Telegram group at [t.me/istanbul_digital_nomads](https://t.me/istanbul_digital_nomads). That's where most of the action happens. You can also follow our GitHub repos for resources and event planning.

**Is this only for developers or tech people?**
Not at all. We welcome anyone who works remotely: writers, designers, marketers, consultants, founders, teachers, and more. If you work from your laptop and you're in Istanbul, you're one of us.

**Do I need to be in Istanbul right now to join?**
No. If you're planning to come to Istanbul or just curious about the nomad scene here, you're welcome to join and ask questions.

## About Istanbul

**Is Istanbul good for digital nomads?**
Yes. The cost of living is affordable compared to most European cities. Internet is fast and widely available. The food is incredible. The city has a huge variety of cafes and coworking spaces. And the culture is endlessly interesting.

**Do I need a visa?**
It depends on your nationality. Many countries get visa-free entry for 30 to 90 days. For longer stays, Turkey offers a residence permit and a digital nomad visa. Check our [resources repo](https://github.com/istanbul-digital-nomads/resources) for details.

**What's the internet like?**
Generally good. Most cafes and coworking spaces offer 50 to 100+ Mbps. Home internet plans with 100+ Mbps are affordable. Mobile data is cheap and reliable with carriers like Turkcell, Vodafone, and Turk Telekom.

**Which neighborhoods are best for nomads?**
Popular choices include Kadikoy (Asian side, artsy and affordable), Besiktas (European side, lively), Cihangir (central, cafe culture), and Moda (relaxed, waterfront). Check our resources repo for a detailed neighborhood guide.

## About Meetups

**How often do meetups happen?**
We aim for at least one meetup per week, usually at a cafe or coworking space. Check the Telegram group for upcoming events.

**Do I have to come to meetups?**
No pressure at all. Come when you can, skip when you can't. There's no attendance requirement.

**Can I organize a meetup?**
Absolutely. Check the [events repo](https://github.com/istanbul-digital-nomads/events) for templates and guidelines. Anyone can propose and organize an event.

## About Contributing

**How can I help?**
Check our [contributing guide](CONTRIBUTING.md) for all the ways you can get involved. The short version: share your knowledge, help newcomers, organize events, or improve our docs.

**I found outdated information in the repos. What should I do?**
Open a pull request with the fix, or open an issue describing what needs updating. We appreciate it!
