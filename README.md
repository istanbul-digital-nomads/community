# Community Guidelines

Welcome to the Istanbul Digital Nomads community. This repo contains everything about how our community works, what we expect from members, and how you can contribute.

- [FAQ](FAQ.md) - Common questions from new members

## Our Values

- **Be welcoming.** Everyone was new to Istanbul at some point. Help people feel at home.
- **Be respectful.** We come from different countries, cultures, and backgrounds. That diversity is our strength.
- **Be helpful.** Share what you know. A small tip about a good cafe or a visa process can make someone's week.
- **Be honest.** Don't promote services or products without being upfront about it. No spam, no scams.
