# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making the Istanbul Digital Nomads community a welcoming, safe, and inclusive space for everyone regardless of nationality, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, age, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Being kind and respectful in all interactions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get settled
- Sharing knowledge, tips, and resources generously
- Respecting different opinions and working styles
- Giving and receiving feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility when you make a mistake and learning from it

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments about someone's nationality, religion, gender, or background
- Spam, unsolicited promotion, or MLM/pyramid scheme recruiting
- Sharing someone's personal information without their consent
- Scams, misleading job offers, or dishonest business practices
- Disruptive behavior at meetups or events
- Any illegal activity

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all community spaces including our Telegram group, GitHub repos, meetups, events, and any other spaces where community members interact.

## Enforcement

Community organizers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. Messaging an admin directly in the Telegram group
2. Opening a private issue in this repo

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

**Consequences for violations:**

- **First offense:** A friendly private reminder about community standards
- **Second offense:** A warning and temporary mute in community channels
- **Serious or repeated violations:** Permanent removal from the community
